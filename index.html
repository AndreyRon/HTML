Yasam- Israel Police Special Patrol Unit

History

Yasam Unit (Hebrew: יס"מ) is the Israel Police Special Patrol Unit (Hebrew: יחידת סיור מיוחדת Yeḥidat Siyur Meyuḥedet), a riot police unit dedicated to continuous security, riot and crowd control, and other special operations.
The first Yasam Unit was established in 1988 with the aim of providing a serious response to the war on terrorism, over the years additional units were founded throughout the country.
Today, a large number of Yasam units operate in Israel, with each unit having its own geographical area, with the threats and tasks typical of its area. For example, a Yasam unit in Jerusalem will not interfere with the work of a Yasam unit that operates in Tel Aviv.

Specialization

  • Counterterrorism
  • Special Reconnaissance
  • Fight against crime
  • Riot and crowd control

Values

  • Protect the lives and property of our citizens and impartially enforce the law.
  • Fight crime, both by preventing it and aggressively pursuing violators of the law.
  • Value human life, respect the dignity of each individual and render our services with courtesy and civility.
  • Maintain a higher standard of integrity, professionalism and fighting spirit.
